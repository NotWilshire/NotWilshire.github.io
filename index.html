Inbound Marketing Precision

At Stellar Development Foundation, I played a pivotal role in refining our inbound marketing strategy. I effectively utilized content funnels, ensuring that our digital presence wasn't just about visibility but about driving actionable leads. During my time at MDWFP, I set up a content-rich blog, strategically driving traffic from social media platforms. This effort was supplemented by organic content that I adapted for an increasingly digital audience. Our monthly newsletter not only kept our community informed but also expanded our CRM footprint.
